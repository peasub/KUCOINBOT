For every ACTIVITY_WARN or missed move:

- identify which blocker family prevented the trade

D. Research harness
Build a separate research stack that can:
- replay your own logs and fills

- stress-test entry timing rules
- score robustness by regime bucket, not only aggregate PnL

E. Execution realism model
Add a sidecar simulator for:
- maker fill probability
- queue-loss probability
- cancel/replace cost
- slippage under market fallback

Tier 3 — Future innovation
F. Market psychology engine
Separate module, not in the hot path initially:
- classify expansion, panic, exhaustion, squeeze, chase, trap, trend acceptance, trend rejection

Use this first as a filter/ranker, not as a direct order engine.

G. ML layer
Only after the trade-quality ledger is large enough.
Use ML for:
- blocker override probability

H. Quantum-inspired ideas

Not for live decision logic.
