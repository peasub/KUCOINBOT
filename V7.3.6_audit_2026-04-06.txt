V7.3.6 CHANGES
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

FIX 1: engine.py — AVG_COST RECOVERY FALLBACK
  BEFORE: recover_avg_cost_if_needed tries fills API once every
  30s. If fills don't match (orphan from double-fill), it returns
  silently. Bot stays stuck forever.
  AFTER: Tracks attempt count. After 10 failed attempts (~5 min),
  falls back to market price as avg_cost. Logs AVG_RECOVER_FALLBACK.
  Logs AVG_RECOVER_PENDING on each attempt for visibility.
  RESULT: Maximum stuck time = 5 minutes instead of infinite.
  The avg may be slightly wrong but the bot can manage the position.

FIX 2: strategy.py — SQMR BBW THRESHOLD
  BEFORE: bbw <= 0.008 (hardcoded). Too tight — SQMR never fired.
  AFTER: Uses orch_vol_exp_bbw_min (0.012) from config.
  RESULT: SQMR can fire when bbw is in the lower 60% of its range.
